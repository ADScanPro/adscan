# Code of Conduct

ADscan welcomes practical collaboration from the security community.

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and direct.
- Keep discussions technical and relevant.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or spam.
- Never publish customer data, credentials, hashes, internal domains, or exploit output from systems you are not authorized to test.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate these rules. Serious or repeated abuse may lead to a ban from project spaces.

Report conduct problems or sensitive concerns to hello@adscanpro.com.
